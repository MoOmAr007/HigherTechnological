About Higher Technological Institute

The First Private Institute In Egypt , It is a co-educational, land-grant, space-grant, research university located in 10th of Ramadan City.

In the eighties (1980-1990), a group of Egyptians - including former ministers, many interested in education, university professors and industrialists - established the Association for the Development of New Urban Communities. It is a non-profit association whose goal is to help the government and the private sector repair vast areas of the Egyptian desert. Soon, this group realized the community’s need for an engineering education of a special nature to be based in these new societies and to meet the needs of the increasing efforts for reconstruction and industrialization in these societies. In 1988, the Higher Technological Institute opened its doors to students as the first private institute of engineering university education in Egypt. The new institute was born strong and very quickly, it has grown in quality and reputation, and now it has been established for more than 30 years and it is still one of the most popular private engineering institutes in Egypt for students of science.

The vision of the Higher Institute of Technology has always been to consolidate its position as a world-class vocational school to which it attracts prominent faculty and bright students. The institute will contribute to the development of Egypt and the entire region by providing education at the highest level and encouraging basic and applied scientific research that reaches the international level.
